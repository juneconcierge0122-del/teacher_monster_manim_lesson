# Landau Mechanics 01 — story sample v2

## 繁體中文旁白

想像我們只看見這顆擺球。它來回擺動，而我們想把它的運動完整記錄下來。

最直接的方法，是每一刻都寫下它的 x 和 y 座標。可是，這兩個數字真的彼此獨立嗎？

擺線長度固定，所以 x 平方加 y 平方永遠等於擺長的平方。知道其中的位置後，另一個就受到限制。

我們其實只需要一個數字：擺線偏離鉛直方向的角度。把這個角度叫做 q。

q 不是新的空間方向。它是為這個系統量身選擇的座標。只要給定 q，擺球的位置就完全確定。

但故事還沒結束。讓擺球兩次經過同一個位置：第一次向左，第二次向右。

如果只知道 q，我們分不出這兩種未來。還必須知道 q 變化得多快，以及變化的方向，也就是 q dot；這個量稱為廣義速度。

因此，單擺只有一個自由度；而要指定某一刻的機械狀態，我們需要 q 和 q dot。這就是廣義座標的第一個力量：用最少、但足夠的資訊描述運動。

## English narration

Imagine that all we can see is this pendulum bob. It swings back and forth, and we want to record its motion completely.

The obvious approach is to write down its x and y coordinates at every instant. But are those two numbers truly independent?

The string has a fixed length, so x squared plus y squared must always equal the length squared. Choosing one position restricts the other coordinate.

In fact, one number is enough: the angle of the string from the vertical. Let us call that angle q.

q is not another direction in space. It is a coordinate chosen to fit this particular system. Once q is known, the position of the bob is fixed.

But that is not the end of the story. Watch the bob pass through the same position twice: first moving left, then moving right.

Knowing q alone cannot distinguish those two futures. We also need the rate and direction at which q is changing: q dot. This quantity is the generalized velocity.

The pendulum therefore has one degree of freedom, while its mechanical state requires both q and q dot. That is the first power of a generalized coordinate: it gives us the smallest complete description of motion.
