# Landau Mechanics 19 — Rutherford's formula: the fingerprint of Coulomb scattering

> 《Landau–Lifshitz 經典力學》教學系列第 19 課
> 中文標題：拉塞福公式：庫侖力的散射指紋

## 繁體中文旁白

現在把散射理論用在庫侖位能 U(r) = α / r；α 的正負分別代表排斥或吸引，但力的大小都依平方反比衰減。

對約化質量 μ、無限遠速度 v∞ 與撞擊參數 b，解出雙曲線軌道後可得 b = [α / (μ × v∞²)] × cot(χ / 2)。

這個關係很直觀：b 越大，粒子離中心越遠，散射角 χ 越小；要產生大角度偏轉，就必須非常靠近力中心。

把 b(χ) 代入一般截面公式，得到 dσ/dΩ = [α / (2 × μ × v∞²)]² / sin⁴(χ / 2)，這就是拉塞福散射公式。

最醒目的特徵是 sin⁻⁴(χ / 2)：小角度事件極多，而大角度散射雖然稀少，卻提供最直接的近距離結構資訊。

公式只含 α²，所以把庫侖力從排斥改成吸引不會改變角分布；軌道彎向相反方向，但相同角度的事件數一致。

若散射中心遠重於入射粒子，μ 幾乎等於入射質量；測量 dσ/dΩ 隨角度與能量的變化，就能檢驗平方反比定律與中心電荷。

## English narration

Apply scattering theory to the Coulomb potential U(r) = α / r. The sign of α distinguishes repulsion from attraction, while the force magnitude follows an inverse-square law.

For reduced mass μ, speed at infinity v∞, and impact parameter b, the hyperbolic orbit gives b = [α / (μ × v∞²)] × cot(χ / 2).

The relation is intuitive: a larger b keeps the particle farther from the centre and produces a smaller scattering angle χ; large deflections require a close encounter.

Substituting b(χ) into the general cross-section gives dσ/dΩ = [α / (2 × μ × v∞²)]² / sin⁴(χ / 2), Rutherford's scattering formula.

Its striking signature is sin⁻⁴(χ / 2): small-angle events are abundant, while rare large-angle events carry the clearest information about short-distance structure.

Only α² appears, so changing the Coulomb force from repulsive to attractive does not change the angular distribution. The orbit bends the other way, but the event count is identical.

When the scattering centre is much heavier than the projectile, μ is nearly the projectile mass. Measuring dσ/dΩ versus angle and energy tests the inverse-square law and central charge.
