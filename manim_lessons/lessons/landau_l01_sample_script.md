# Landau Mechanics 01 — Sample narration

## zh-TW

學習目標是理解自由度、廣義座標與廣義速度。先備知識只有三角函數、位置與速度。

要描述單擺，真的需要同時追蹤 x 和 y 嗎？兩個座標受到擺長固定這一個約束，所以它們並不獨立。一個角度就能決定整個位置。

廣義座標是能唯一描述系統形狀的獨立變數。我們把 q 選成擺角 theta。幾何關係把角度轉成直角座標。對時間微分，廣義速度 q dot 就決定實際速度。

思考：若只知道擺角 q，能預測它下一秒往哪裡走嗎？不能；同一位置可能向左或向右，還需要廣義速度。

自由度是描述構形所需的獨立數目。廣義座標不必是直角座標。位置與速度一起決定機械狀態。

## en

The goal is to understand degrees of freedom, generalized coordinates, and generalized velocities. The prerequisites are trigonometry, position, and velocity.

Do we really need both x and y to describe a pendulum? The two coordinates obey one fixed-length constraint, so they are not independent. One angle determines the entire position.

A generalized coordinate is an independent variable that fixes the configuration. We choose q to be the angle theta. Geometry converts the angle into Cartesian coordinates. Differentiate in time: generalized velocity q dot fixes the physical velocity.

Think: if you know only q, can you predict which way the bob moves next? No. At the same position it may move left or right; velocity is also needed.

A degree of freedom is the number of independent configuration variables. A generalized coordinate need not be Cartesian. Position and velocity together specify the mechanical state.
