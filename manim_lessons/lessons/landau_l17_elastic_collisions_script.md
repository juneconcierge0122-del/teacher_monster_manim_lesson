# Landau Mechanics 17 — Elastic collisions: only direction changes in the centre-of-mass frame

> 《Landau–Lifshitz 經典力學》教學系列第 17 課
> 中文標題：彈性碰撞：質心系裡只轉方向

## 繁體中文旁白

彈性碰撞不改變粒子的內部狀態，因此總動量與動能在碰撞前後都保持不變。

在質心系中，兩粒子的動量始終大小相等、方向相反；碰撞只把這對向量旋轉，長度完全不變。

回到實驗室系，只要把質心速度 V 加回兩個碰後速度；守恆律決定可能的速度圓，但散射方向仍取決於粒子間作用力。

若第二粒子起初靜止，它最多取得入射能量的 4m₁m₂/(m₁+m₂)²；兩個質量相等時，能量可以完全轉移。

## English narration

An elastic collision leaves the particles' internal states unchanged, so both total momentum and kinetic energy are conserved.

In the centre-of-mass frame the two momenta remain equal and opposite; the collision merely rotates this pair of vectors without changing their lengths.

Returning to the laboratory means adding the centre-of-mass velocity V to both final velocities. Conservation fixes a velocity circle, while the interaction selects the scattering direction.

If the second particle starts at rest, it can receive at most the fraction 4m₁m₂/(m₁+m₂)² of the incident energy; equal masses allow complete transfer.
