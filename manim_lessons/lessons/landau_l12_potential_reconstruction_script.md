# Landau Mechanics 12 — Reconstructing a potential well from its period

> 《Landau–Lifshitz 經典力學》教學系列第 12 課
> 中文標題：由週期反推勢能井

## 繁體中文旁白

只量到不同能量下的振盪週期，能不能反推出粒子所在的勢能井？

對每個能量 E，水平線會在兩個轉向點 x₁ 和 x₂ 碰到 U(x)；兩點距離就是振盪寬度。

阿貝爾反演把週期資料變成寬度：x₂(U) − x₁(U) = [1/(π√{2m})]∫₀ᵁ T(E)/√(U−E) dE。

週期只能決定勢能井的寬度，不能決定左右形狀；再假設勢能對稱，才得到唯一的 U(x)。

## English narration

If we measure only the oscillation period at different energies, can we reconstruct the particle's potential well?

For each energy E, a horizontal line meets U(x) at two turning points, x one and x two; their separation is the oscillation width.

Abel inversion converts period data into width: x₂(U) − x₁(U) = [1/(π√{2m})]∫₀ᵁ T(E)/√(U−E) dE.

The period fixes the width, but not the separate left and right shapes; assuming a symmetric well makes U(x) unique.
