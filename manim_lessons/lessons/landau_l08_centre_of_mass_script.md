# Landau Mechanics 08 — The centre of mass: one point for a whole system

> 《Landau–Lifshitz 經典力學》教學系列第 8 課
> 中文標題：質心：把整個系統看成一點

## 繁體中文旁白

兩個粒子互相碰撞時，能不能找到一個更簡單的代表點？

把每個位置乘上它的質量，再除以總質量，就得到質心 R。

內力成對抵消，所以質心只受到外力：M R̈ = F 外。

複雜系統的整體平移，可以先由一個質心描述。

## English narration

When particles collide, can one representative point simplify the picture?

Multiply each position by its mass and divide by total mass: that gives the centre of mass R.

Internal forces cancel in pairs, so only external force moves it: M R̈ = F external.

The translation of a complicated system can be described first by one centre of mass.
