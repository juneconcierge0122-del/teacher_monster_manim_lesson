# Landau Mechanics 11 — One-dimensional motion: read the trajectory from energy

> 《Landau–Lifshitz 經典力學》教學系列第 11 課
> 中文標題：一維運動：從能量讀出軌跡

## 繁體中文旁白

只有一個自由度時，能不能不先解牛頓方程，就知道粒子怎麼走？

對座標 x，L = ½mẋ² − U(x)，而能量 E = ½mẋ² + U(x) 保持不變。

因此速度由能量差決定：ẋ = ±√{2[E−U(x)]/m}；只有 U(x) ≤ E 的區域允許運動。

當 U(x) = E，速度降為零，粒子在轉向點折返；兩個轉向點之間就是有限振盪。

## English narration

With only one degree of freedom, can we predict the motion without first solving Newton's equation?

For coordinate x, L = ½mẋ² − U(x), while the conserved energy is E = ½mẋ² + U(x).

The energy difference sets the speed: ẋ = ±√{2[E−U(x)]/m}; motion is allowed only where U(x) ≤ E.

Where U(x) = E, the speed vanishes and the particle turns back; two turning points bound an oscillation.
