# Landau Mechanics 06 — Energy: the conserved quantity from uniform time

> 《Landau–Lifshitz 經典力學》教學系列第 6 課
> 中文標題：能量：時間均勻性留下的守恆量

## 繁體中文旁白

為什麼有些數字會在運動中保持不變？

如果物理定律今天和明天完全相同，L 就不顯含時間。

把 L 對速度的導數乘上速度，再減去 L，得到 E = Σ q̇∂L/∂q̇ − L。

這個 E 就是能量；時間的均勻性，變成了能量守恆。

## English narration

Why do some quantities stay constant during motion?

If the laws of physics are the same today and tomorrow, L has no explicit time dependence.

Multiply each velocity by its derivative of L, then subtract L: E = Σ q̇∂L/∂q̇ − L.

This E is energy. Uniformity of time becomes conservation of energy.
