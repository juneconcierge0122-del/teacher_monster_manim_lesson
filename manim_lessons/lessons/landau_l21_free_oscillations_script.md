# Landau Mechanics 21 — Free oscillations in one dimension: every stable equilibrium is harmonic

> 《Landau–Lifshitz 經典力學》教學系列第 21 課
> 中文標題：一維自由振盪：每個穩定平衡都是簡諧

## 繁體中文旁白

力學系統最常見的運動之一，就是在穩定平衡附近的小振盪；我們先看最簡單的一個自由度。

穩定平衡對應位能的極小值；在附近把位能展開，最低階就是一個二次項，係數 k 正是位能在該點的二階導數。

動能近似為二分之一 m 乘上速度平方，於是這個系統的拉格朗日量就是動能減位能。

代入運動方程，得到一條線性方程：質量乘加速度，加上 k 乘位移等於零，也就是加速度正比於負的位移。

它的解是一個簡諧振盪：位移隨時間做餘弦擺動，振幅是 a，相位裡的 α 是初相位。

關鍵是角頻率只由系統本身決定：等於根號 k 除以 m，和振幅、初始條件都無關。

總能量是動能加位能，算出來正比於振幅的平方，等於二分之一 m 乘上角頻率平方再乘上振幅平方。

## English narration

One of the most common motions in mechanics is a small oscillation about a stable equilibrium; we begin with the simplest case, a single degree of freedom.

Stable equilibrium sits at a minimum of the potential energy; expanding around it, the leading term is quadratic, with coefficient k equal to the second derivative there.

The kinetic energy is approximately one-half m times velocity squared, so the Lagrangian of the system is the kinetic minus the potential energy.

Putting this into the equation of motion gives a linear equation: mass times acceleration plus k times displacement equals zero, so the acceleration is proportional to minus the displacement.

Its solution is a harmonic oscillation: the displacement swings as a cosine in time, with amplitude a and an initial phase alpha.

The key point is that the angular frequency is fixed by the system alone: it is the square root of k over m, independent of amplitude or initial conditions.

The total energy is kinetic plus potential, and it works out proportional to the amplitude squared, equal to one-half m times angular frequency squared times amplitude squared.

旁白為自然語言，數學式以放大字體顯示於畫面中央（按旁白行對應）：

- 第 2 句 / line 2: `U(x) ≈ ½ k x²        (k > 0)`
- 第 3 句 / line 3: `L = ½ m ẋ² − ½ k x²`
- 第 4 句 / line 4: `m ẍ + k x = 0    →    ẍ + ω² x = 0`
- 第 5 句 / line 5: `x = a · cos(ω t + α)`
- 第 6 句 / line 6: `ω = √( k / m )`
- 第 7 句 / line 7: `E = ½ m ω² a²`
