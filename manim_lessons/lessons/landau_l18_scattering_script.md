# Landau Mechanics 18 — Scattering: from impact parameter to cross-section

> 《Landau–Lifshitz 經典力學》教學系列第 18 課
> 中文標題：散射：從撞擊參數到截面

## 繁體中文旁白

守恆律只能限制碰撞結果；若要算出粒子究竟偏轉多少，還必須知道中心位能 U(r) 並解出軌道。

把兩體碰撞化成約化質量 μ 的單粒子問題後，入射軌道由無限遠速度 v∞ 與撞擊參數 b 描述。

撞擊參數 b 是入射直線與力中心的垂直距離；角動量是 M = μ × b × v∞，能量是 E = (1/2) × μ × v∞²。

粒子先接近中心，在最近距離 rₘᵢₙ 轉向後離開；軌道的對稱性把入射與出射漸近線連到散射角 χ。

不同的 b 對應不同的 χ：小撞擊參數通常靠中心更近、偏轉更大，大撞擊參數則幾乎沿原方向前進。

對均勻粒子束，落在 b 到 b + db 圓環內的粒子數與面積 2π × b × db 成正比，因此散射截面 dσ = 2π × b × db。

把 b 寫成 χ 的函數，就得到微分截面 dσ/dΩ = [b / sin(χ)] × |db/dχ|；它把可測的角分布直接連回作用位能。

## English narration

Conservation laws only constrain a collision. To calculate the actual deflection, we must know the central potential U(r) and solve the orbit.

After reducing the two-body collision to one particle of reduced mass μ, the incoming orbit is described by its speed at infinity v∞ and impact parameter b.

The impact parameter b is the perpendicular distance from the incoming line to the force centre, giving M = μ × b × v∞ and E = (1/2) × μ × v∞².

The particle approaches the centre, turns at its closest distance rₘᵢₙ, and escapes. Orbit symmetry connects the incoming and outgoing asymptotes to the scattering angle χ.

Different values of b produce different χ: a small impact parameter usually means a closer encounter and larger deflection, while a large b gives almost straight motion.

For a uniform beam, particles between b and b + db occupy an annulus of area 2π × b × db, defining the cross-section dσ = 2π × b × db.

Writing b as a function of χ gives dσ/dΩ = [b / sin(χ)] × |db/dχ|. This differential cross-section links the measured angular distribution back to the interaction potential.
