# Landau Mechanics 24 — Vibrations of molecules: counting and normal modes

> 《Landau–Lifshitz 經典力學》教學系列第 24 課
> 中文標題：分子振動：從自由度到簡正模態

## 繁體中文旁白

分子裡的原子不只會在平衡位置附近振動；整個分子還能平移和轉動。

n 個原子共有 3n 個自由度；扣掉 3 個平移和 3 個轉動，剩下 3n−6 個才是振動。

直線分子是例外：繞著分子軸的轉動沒有意義，只有 2 個轉動自由度，所以有 3n−5 個振動。

要分離出振動，就令總動量為零讓質心固定，再令總角動量為零把整體轉動去掉。

以直線三原子分子為例；第一個簡正模態是對稱伸縮：兩端原子對稱地一起進出，中間原子不動。

第二個是反對稱伸縮：兩端原子往同一方向移動，中間原子往反方向移動，頻率比較高。

第三個是彎曲模態：原子垂直於軸擺動、讓分子彎折；它在兩個互相垂直的平面裡簡併，對應兩個相同的頻率。

## English narration

The atoms in a molecule do not only vibrate about equilibrium; the whole molecule can also translate and rotate.

With n atoms there are 3n degrees of freedom; removing 3 translations and 3 rotations leaves 3n − 6 vibrations.

A linear molecule is the exception: rotation about the molecular axis is meaningless, leaving only 2 rotations and therefore 3n − 5 vibrations.

To isolate the vibrations we set the total momentum to zero, fixing the centre of mass, and the total angular momentum to zero, removing overall rotation.

Take a linear triatomic molecule; the first normal mode is the symmetric stretch, where the outer atoms move in and out together while the centre stays still.

The second is the antisymmetric stretch, where the outer atoms move the same way and the centre moves opposite, at a higher frequency.

The third is the bending mode, where the atoms move across the axis and the molecule bends; it is doubly degenerate, one for each of two perpendicular planes.

## 動畫 / Animation

- 直線三原子分子 A–B–A（外側 m₁ 兩顆、中央 m₂ 一顆）。
