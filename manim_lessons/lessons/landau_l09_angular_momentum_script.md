# Landau Mechanics 09 — Angular momentum: the conserved quantity of rotation

> 《Landau–Lifshitz 經典力學》教學系列第 9 課
> 中文標題：角動量：旋轉的守恆量

## 繁體中文旁白

直線平移有動量，那旋轉運動有什麼對應的數字？

從原點到粒子的向量 r，和動量 p 做叉積，得到角動量 M = r × p。

若外力矩為零，角動量的時間導數就是零。

旋轉對稱性，留下角動量守恆。

## English narration

Linear motion has momentum. What is its counterpart for rotation?

Take the cross product of position r with momentum p: angular momentum M = r × p.

When the external torque is zero, its time derivative vanishes.

Rotational symmetry leaves us conservation of angular momentum.
