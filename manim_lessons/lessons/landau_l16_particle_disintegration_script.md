# Landau Mechanics 16 — Particle disintegration: conservation predicts the fragments

> 《Landau–Lifshitz 經典力學》教學系列第 16 課
> 中文標題：粒子解體：守恆律預測碎片方向

## 繁體中文旁白

一個靜止粒子自發解體成兩個粒子時，不必知道作用力細節，動量與能量守恆就能預測結果。

在質心系中，兩個碎片帶著大小相等、方向相反的動量；若解體能為 ε，則 p₀ = √(2με)。

若母粒子在實驗室中以速度 V 運動，碎片速度只是向量相加 v = V + v₀；質心系的速度球因此整體平移。

當 V > v₀，碎片只能向前落在一個圓錐內；若最大張角是 θₘₐₓ，則 sin(θₘₐₓ) = v₀ / V。

## English narration

When a particle at rest spontaneously splits into two, momentum and energy conservation predict the outcome without details of the interaction.

In the centre-of-mass frame the fragments have equal and opposite momenta; for disintegration energy ε, their magnitude is p₀ = √(2με).

If the parent moves through the laboratory with velocity V, each fragment velocity is simply v = V + v₀, translating the centre-of-mass velocity sphere.

When V > v₀, a fragment can emerge only inside a forward cone. If its maximum angle is θₘₐₓ, then sin(θₘₐₓ) = v₀ / V.
